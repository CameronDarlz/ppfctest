About the Club

Penmaenmawr Phoenix FC is a Welsh football club based in Penmaenmawr, Conwy County Borough, Wales. We’re proud of our community roots and our growing junior section.

  • Community

Quick facts: Ground capacity ~550 (50 seated). Chairman: Peter Williams. Manager: Az Keating.
